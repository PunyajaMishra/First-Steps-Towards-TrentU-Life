Step 2: Activate your Trent account and myTrent Portal

Trent account and myTrent Portal

The myTrent portal gives you access to academic records, email, learning system, news, events, information and much more.

Activating your Trent account is very simple, If you have applied to Trent, or have received an Offer of Admission, you can activate your MyTrent account here.

You will need your Trent Identification Number provided in your application acknowledgement email. If you are unable to find your ID, you can retrieve it here OR please contact admissions@trentu.ca.

Class Registration Using myTrent For Undergraduate Applicants and Students

For Class Registration To register for classes, you will need to first accept your Offer of Admission.

  • Students entering Trent in September will be able to use their account to begin registering for classes after June 25.
  • Students entering Trent in January will be able to begin registering for classes within one week of accepting their Offer of Admission.
  • Students entering Trent in Spring session will be able to use their account to begin registering for classes by early March.

Class Registration Using myTrentFor Graduate Students

For Class Registration To register for classes, you will need to first accept your Offer of Admission.

  • Graduate Students will be able to see the status of their admission once they log in to their mytrent account
  • Graduate Students are encouraged to view required documents under academics tab on their mytrent account to
    see what documents we have received and which ones are still required to be submitted. Please note only complete applications are forwarded to the program. Applicants are advised to ensure that all required documents are submitted.
  • Graduate Students entering Trent will be able to register in courses 6 weeks prior to their start date.

Once activated you can access your myTrent portal from here.
