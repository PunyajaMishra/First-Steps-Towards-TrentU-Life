Step 2: Activating your Trent account and myTrent Portal

Trent account and myTrent Portal

The myTrent portal gives you access to academic records, email, Blackboard, news, events, information and much more.

Activating your Trent account is very simple. If you have applied to Trent or have received an Offer of Admission, you can activate your myTrent account here.

You will need your Trent Identification Number provided in your application acknowledgement email. If you are unable to find your ID, you can retrieve it here or contact admissions@trentu.ca.

Class Registration using myTrent: Undergraduate Applicants and Students

To register for classes, you will need to first accept your Offer of Admission.

  • Students entering Trent in September will be able to register for courses starting June 26.
  • Students entering Trent in January will be able to register for courses within one week of accepting their Offer of Admission.
  • Students entering Trent in the Spring term will be able to register for courses in early March.

Class Registration using myTrent: Graduate Students

To register for classes, you will need to first accept your Offer of Admission.

  • Graduate students will be able to see the status of their admission once they log into their myTrent account
  • Graduate students are encouraged to view required documents under the "Academics" tab on their myTrent account. Please note, only completed applications are forwarded to the program. Applicants are advised to ensure that all required documents are submitted.
  • Graduate students entering Trent will be able to register for courses 6 weeks prior to their start date.

Once activated, you can access your myTrent portal here.
