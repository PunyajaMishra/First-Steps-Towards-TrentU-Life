Lets get the part started!
Orientation week is the first week of your school. Here you come to know about TRENT and what it has to offer. In this period you also get to meet a lot of people, make new friends, enjoy many activities, learn a lot more about different clubs and groups and about your college. This is week is loads of fun. CONSENT At TRENT

At Trent University, we want to create a Culture of Consent. That means respecting people's boundaries, communicating about consent, not coercing or pressuring people to do what they don't want to do, and standing up against sexual violence.
