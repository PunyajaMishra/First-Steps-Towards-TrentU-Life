Step 10: Move Into Residence

What will you need!
So to move into Residence you need to go ahead make sure that you have something ready before you actually can move in. Here is a list of all the thing that you need to know.

  • Check In QR code
  • Student ID
  • Residence Key
  • Move In timeslot

Check In QR code

Move in QR code is basically a QR that you get when you have applied, chosen and accepted the residence you want to stay in.

When you arrive you would need to Check in first to indicate your arrival and to pick up the keys for the residence.

After that you can move the location of the residence to move your stuff into the residence.

To know about the direction click on the link CLICK HERE

Student ID

You Student ID card acts as a tap login for many facilities on the campus. It is a also the tap login for some of the residences and some of the commons spaces.

You can get your student ID from the Blackburn Hall.Click here to get the location.

You would need a picture for the student ID. You can either upload a picture of your choice by logining in MY Trent or one can be click for you when you come to blackburn hall.

Residence Key

This the key for your residence main gate and for personal or shared rooms.

To get the keys you need to first check In using the QR code provided and then need to move up to the Main campus to pick up the keys from the Check In booth.

On move in day you will find leaders and volunteers and they will be able to help you to reach the Check In booth.

Move-in Timeslot

In order to safely accommodate over 1,800 students moving into residence Student Housing has extended the move-in period over two days, from Saturday, September 3 through Sunday, September 4. Each student has been assigned to a timeslot to help us manage a safe and orderly welcome to the University.

Move-in time slots have a duration of two hours before the next group of students arrives, so please plan to arrive within two hours of the time indicated via email.

If you are unable to arrive during your scheduled move-in time slot, please contact Student Housing at residence@trentu.ca before you arrive on campus and we will work with you to accommodate your needs.
