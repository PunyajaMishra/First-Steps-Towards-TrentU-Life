Step 07: Trent International Welcome Session

Overview

Trent International Welcome Session is an online session to help internationl students know more about the resources availabel on campus in general and resources specifically available for international students. This session is for Oncoming and new Students at Trent. There are many informative session that keep on happening to help students more inforation about know more about Canadian Laws and help them make the Transition easier.

Register here

Seminars

Name Description
ASK AN IMMIGRATION ADVISOR - LIVE CHAT Have immigration questions relating to study permits, entry visas, working conditions, course-load requirements, or the Post-Graduation Work Permit Program? Join this immigration live chat!
IMMIGRATION 101 Come learn the basics of immigration for international students at Trent. This information session will provide basic information on your immigration documents, academics and immigration, study permit working conditions, the Post-Graduation Work Permit Program, and more.
STUDY PERMIT WORKING CONDITIONS Did you know that as international student in Canada, you may be eligible to work? Come learn more about working conditions eligibility, the importance of full-time enrollment, summer work, and more.
