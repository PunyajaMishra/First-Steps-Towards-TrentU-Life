Step 01: Study Permit

Congratulations for accepting the offer letter with Trent University!
As an international student, before you can come to Canada, you need to apply for a study permit.

What is a Study Permit?

The study permit is the legal document issued by Government of Canada that allows the foreign nationals to study at a designated leanring institutions in Canada.

Your study permit is not a visa. It doesnâ€™t let you enter Canada. You may also need a visitor visa or an electronic travel authorization (eTA). If your study permit is approved, then a Visa is issued with your study permit.

Do you require a study permit?

A study permit is required for programs of study that are longer than 6 months in duration.

Gather Important Documents

You need the following important documents to apply for a study permit:

  • Proof of Acceptance - Letter of Acceptance from Trent University
  • Proof of Identity - Passport
  • Proof of Financial Support
