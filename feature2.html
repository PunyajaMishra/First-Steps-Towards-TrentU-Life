Study Permit: How to Apply

Congratulations for acceting the offer letter with Trent University! As an international student, before you can come to Canada, you need to apply for a study permit.

What is a Study Permit?

The study permit is the legal document issued by Government of Canada that allows the foreign nationals to study at a designated leanring institutions in Canada.

Do you require a study permit?

A study permit is required for programs of study that are longer than 6 months in duration.

Gather Important Documents

You need the following important documents to apply for a study permit:

  1. Proof of Acceptance - Letter of Acceptance from Trent University
  2. Proof of Identity - Passport
  3. Proof of Financial Support
