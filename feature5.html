Step 4: Housing

Applying to housing might seem hard at times. Luckily, Trent makes it easy. Simply follow this link and sign in using your Trent login information. You will be brought to the on-campus residence application page. A full overview of applying to residence can be found here. For more info about residence, please see the info below.

On-Campus

Peterborough

At Peterborough, Trent has a range of different colleges (housing complexes) to choose from. Whether you want to be close to classes and the water, have your own kitchen and common room, or are looking for graduate housing, Trent has options for you.

Additionally each college has what's called living learning communities. Each community focuses on certain aspects, such as a love for creativity or environmental sustainability. The idea behind this is that your roomates will share some common interests and values that might help you make friends easier and get you involved in events you might like. More info can be found here.

Durham

Trent has one housing location available for those studying at the Durham campus. Info can be found here.

Off-Campus

Whether it be Peterborough or Durham, there are a number of websites to use to help find a place to stay during your studies. Some include:

  • Places4Students

  • PadMapper

A more comprehensive list can be found here.

HomeShare

HomeShare is a program that matches students (both full and part-time) with older adults (age 55+) with an extra bedroom. In exchange for a reduced rent cost ($400 - $600 a month) students perform up to 7 hours of light work (walking pets, helping with groceries, etc..) for the adult. This process can take 1 - 3 months, features an enhanced criminal record check, and a reference check. For more information please click here.
