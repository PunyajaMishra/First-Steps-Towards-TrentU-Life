WELCOME INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

To a Place you can call Home

Welcome to Trent University Family! Thank you for accepting the offer letter. We are excited for you to start your journey here at Trent and want to make your transition as smooth as possible. You are now officially part of Trent University. We are here to assist you with all and any questions you have.

As an international student, the onboarding process is a little different and longer than that of a domestic student's. There are a few extra processes and procedures that needs to be followed to ensure a smooth start of University Life. But not to worry, we have created this step-by-step guide for you to follow and ensure you do not miss any step! All the initial steps required have been detailed below.

We have broken down this long process in a game-like structure where as long as you complete each level successfully, you will begin your university at ease and very soon!
Click on each level on the map below and it will take you to a new page with detailed information about the procedure. There are resourcess and links attached at every level for you to refer to. There is also a checklist document you can download if you wish to track your progress locally.

Please feel free to reach out to "Trent International Department" via email with any questions or concerns you have.
