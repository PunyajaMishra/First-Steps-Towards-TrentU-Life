Trent International Orientation

Review the Trent International Student Guides

Trent provides guides for new international students. Some of the contents include working in Canada, first weeks at Trent, student life, and Trent International. Two guides are prepared for the Peterborough and Durham campus (found on the header bar above).

Online Welcome Sessions

Trent offers online (Zoom) welcome sessions in December for new international students. The session will discuss Trent International and the services they provide, health insurance, and study abroad basics. You must register for these sessions before hand, a link can be found here.

In-Person Orientation

Trent offers a few different orientations for if you are at Peterborough or Durham and if you are an undergraduate or graduate student. Please select the one that applies to you.

CONSENT At TRENT

At Trent University, we want to create a Culture of Consent. That means respecting people's boundaries, communicating about consent, not coercing or pressuring people to do what they don't want to do, and standing up against sexual violence.
